About Sandra

Hi, I'm Sandra! I'm a creative professional with a background in video editing, social media management, and web content design. Over the past few years, I've worked with different brands and agencies across Canada, the US, and Australia, helping them grow their presence online through engaging visuals, campaigns, and storytelling.

I'm the type of person who enjoys both the creative and technical sides of digital work. Whether it's editing podcasts and videos, designing a Shopify page, or running social media campaigns, I like bringing ideas to life and making content that people connect with.

I'd say my strengths are being resourceful, adaptable, and always curious. I love learning new tools and trends that make content stand out. At the end of the day, I just enjoy creating things that make an impact, whether it's for a brand, a business, or a community.

What I Do Best

  • Video and podcast editing
  • Social media campaigns and management
  • Web content design (Shopify, landing pages)
  • Visual storytelling and brand campaigns
